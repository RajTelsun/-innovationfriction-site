Instrument V · Crisis Diagnostic

Crisis Diagnostic

30 minutes. Use immediately when something has gone wrong.

Use this now. Do not wait until the board meeting. Do not wait until you have a full picture. The diagnostic takes 30 minutes and gives you the one thing you need in a crisis: clarity about what layer has re-activated and whether the correct response is to eliminate, pivot, or change path.

Step 1 · What type of crisis are you in?
🔴
Customer Lost / Churn
Key customer cancelled, churned, failed to renew, or stalled at adoption after purchase
🔴
Funding Gap / Capital
Funding round failed, investor withdrew, runway shorter than required to reach next milestone
🔴
Technology Failure
Product under-delivered vs promise, technical capability gap discovered, integration failed
🔴
Market / Pivot Signal
Pipeline dried up, conversion rate collapsed, questioning whether the problem is real
