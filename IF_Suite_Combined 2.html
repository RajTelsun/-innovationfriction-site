The four denominator pillars
μhuman
Human Resistance
Psychology, loss aversion, career risk, cultural inertia. Reduced by reference customers and risk-sharing commercial models.
Nlegacy
Legacy Normal Force
Incumbent systems, vendor contracts, regulatory validations. The structural weight your technology must displace or route around.
τcycle
Temporal Friction
Sales cycle × approval layers. Every month of cycle consumed is a month of capital burned. τ and capital are coupled variables.
Ccapital
Capital Friction
Total capital required to reach cash-flow positive. When C_required ÷ C_available approaches 1.0, the company fails regardless of all else.

    Instrument IV · Corporate Innovation Canvas

    Corporate Innovation Canvas

    For innovators inside large organisations navigating internal friction.

    The corporate context is different. The dominant friction is internal, not external. Budget cycle dependency, executive sponsor risk, internal champion exposure, and the politics of build vs buy vs partner — these are the failure layers that corporate innovators face. The IF denominator applies here: μ_human is your executive committee's risk aversion; N_legacy is incumbent processes and vendor relationships; τ_cycle is your budget approval timeline; C_capital is the internal budget you must defend.
