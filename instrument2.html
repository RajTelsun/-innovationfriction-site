Complete this before committing capital, hiring, or making any irreversible decision.

This instrument takes 2–3 hours if completed honestly. The cost of skipping it can be 3 years of your life and several hundred thousand euros.

05The Five Honest QuestionsPre-flight Checklist
Q1
Grandma Test — can a non-technical person repeat your value in 30 seconds without a clarifying question?
If no: μ_human is elevated at every commercial touchpoint. Fix before any customer meeting.
Q2
Does your Beach Head customer have urgent, funded pain today — with confirmed budget authority?
If no: you are at Layer 1 failure. No sales execution overcomes a problem the market does not urgently fund.
Q3
Does your TAM justify the chosen path? (>€1B reachable in 7 years for venture-backed startup)
If smaller: Path A or C may be structurally more appropriate than a venture-backed startup.
Q4
Can a single customer derive full standalone value — no ecosystem dependency?
If no: every additional required party multiplies all four denominator components. Requires ecosystem strategy.
Q5
Does current capital cover the path to CRL 5 (first real revenue) with meaningful buffer?
If no: C_required ÷ C_available approaching 1.0 means the company dies before first revenue regardless of technology quality.
