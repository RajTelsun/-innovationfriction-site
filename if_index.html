The four denominator pillars — what the instruments measure and help you reduce
μhuman
Human Resistance
Psychology, loss aversion, career risk, cultural inertia. Reduced by reference customers, narrative clarity, and risk-sharing commercial models.
Nlegacy
Legacy Normal Force
Incumbent systems, vendor contracts, regulatory validations. The structural weight your technology must displace — or route around via beachhead selection.
τcycle
Temporal Friction
Sales cycle × approval layers. Every month of cycle consumed is a month of capital burned. τ and capital are directly coupled variables.
Ccapital
Capital Friction
Total capital required to reach cash-flow positive. When C_required ÷ C_available approaches 1.0, the company fails regardless of all other variables.
